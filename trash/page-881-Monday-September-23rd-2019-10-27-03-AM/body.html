Power

WordStar is powerful. You can, for instance, type “@” instead of a long name used commonly in your work. Then, when you’re ready, you can plug the name in where the “@” appears. This 306search-and-replace feature is common to every advanced word-processing program; but WordStar implements it better than many rivals.
