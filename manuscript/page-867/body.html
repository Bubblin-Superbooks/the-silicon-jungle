But ragged right margins, the normal typewriter kind, guide the eye more easily and may be better for long reports as opposed to short letters. I did not justify this manuscript. But justification is just the ticket for correspondence with the status conscious.

In the case of all these special features, keep remembering to ask:

  • Does the printer offer them no matter what computer or program you use?
  • If not, will your hardware and software let you use the features?
  • For free, will the store modify your computer system to make the features available to you?
  • Will your desired combinations of features work simultaneously? Unfortunately, with my software, anyway, the Microprism 480 couldn’t use boldface
