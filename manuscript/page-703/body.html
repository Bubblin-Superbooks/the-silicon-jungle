How Easy Is The Network To Install?

A WEB-type network might be a nightmare for the lazy and sloppy. It requires hooking printed circuit boards up to the right leads of chips in the Kaypro. And you must also solder the telephone-style sockets into which your computer’s network cord plugs.

But a good data-processing department shouldn’t have any problems, according to The WEB’s makers.
